Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know

What makes Richline different from rideshare?+
Richline delivers a luxury, private, chauffeur-driven experience with new vehicles, consistent professionalism, on-time guarantees, and flight tracking. Unlike rideshare, you always know your driver, your vehicle, and your exact cost ahead of time.
Do you offer airport pickups at San Diego International (SAN)?+
Yes. We specialize in airport transfers, including SAN, LAX, CRQ, and regional airports. Your chauffeur tracks your arriving flight, adjusts for delays, and meets you at the designated pickup area.
What types of vehicles do you offer?+
Our fleet includes late-model luxury SUVs, executive sedans, and premium Sprinters, all maintained to the highest standard of safety and cleanliness.
Is your pricing transparent?+
Yes. Final pricing is displayed before confirming your reservation — no surge pricing, no hidden fees. Your quote includes taxes, tolls, and standard wait time for airport pickups.
How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak travel times, but we can often accommodate last-minute reservations depending on availability.
